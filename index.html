Experience

Next Generation 911

During the summer of 2014, I worked as an undergraduate research assistant for UNT's Network Security Lab. There, I helped develop next generation 911 software. NG911 is a mobile application for iPhone and Android that helps users properly perform CPR. A web portal is also available, allowing 911 operators or EMS personnel to monitor CPR performance. The American Heart Association's CPR guidelines and guidance from Action EMS were used to inform all decisions made during development.
